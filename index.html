About Us


Home Line Interior Architecture and Design Ltd specialise in the creation, design and interior architecture of exquisite environments for residential interiors at home.


Established in 2016 and based in the Telangana, Home Line has become a leading Architectural Interior Design practice with offices Hyderabad


With Home Line all-encompassing and pragmatic approach to Interior design, an expertise based on accurate spatial planning and improving the flow of the interior space. Through structure, detailed design, the use of lighting and individually designed furniture, fixtures and fittings, the practice has completed a wide variety of projects both at home and abroad.
